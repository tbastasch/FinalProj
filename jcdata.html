Conclusion

Regionally, it seems as though Latin America is embracing Jesuit Institutions while North America and Southern Asia have an established Jesuit Presence (extrapolated from the high Alumni/Instutional numbers for Asia and North America versus the High Undergrad population in Latin America).

From the Country perspective, the U.S has a strong emerging and established Jesuit presence while India has the most institutions established.

Part of the appeal of attending a Jesuit University is the balanced student to faculty ratio. Schools such as Gonzaga (around a 4:1 ratio) or ESADE are preferrable over UNISINOS.

Limitations

The data had many unreported, missing or incorrect values - many times staff was reported without undergrad or vice versa. Other issues included symbols in instution names and N/A values.
